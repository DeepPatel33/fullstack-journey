Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish.

It's creamy, delicious, and surprisingly easy to make!

Cooking Time

Preparation: 10 minutes

Cooking: 15 minutes

Total: 25 minutes

Ingredients

400g spaghetti

200g bacon or pancetta

4 large eggs

100g parmesan cheese

Salt and black pepper

2 cloves of garlic

Instructions

Step 1: Boil water and cook the spaghetti.

Step 2: Fry the bacon until crispy.

Step 3: Beat the eggs with parmesan cheese.

Step 4: Mix everything together while hot.

Step 5: Season with salt and pepper.

Step 6: Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips

Don't overcook the pasta.

The heat from the pasta will cook the eggs.

Add pasta water if it's too thick.
